Crown Product Galaxy - Category Images
======================================

Place category artwork images here with these filenames:

- upvc-pressure.png  (UPVC Pressure Pipes & Fittings)
- upvc-drainage.png  (UPVC Drainage Pipes & Fittings)
- upvc-duct.png      (UPVC Duct Pipes & Accessories)
- pvc-conduit.png    (PVC Conduit Pipes)
- ppr.png            (PPR Pipes)
- hdpe.png           (HDPE Pipe Systems)

Recommended dimensions: 200x200px or 400x400px (square, centered artwork)
Format: PNG with transparency for best results on dark background

The component will fall back to category icons if images are missing.
