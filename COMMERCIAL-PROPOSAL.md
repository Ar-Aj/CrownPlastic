## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Crown Plastic Pipes requires a modern, comprehensive corporate website to showcase their full range of PVC/UPVC pipe products, technical resources, and establish a strong digital presence in the Dubai and UAE market.

**Objectives:**
- Present 7 product families with detailed specifications and technical data
- Provide easy access to catalogues, datasheets, and installation guides
- Enable customers to request quotations and contact sales via multiple channels (WhatsApp, email, forms)
- Establish credibility through standards compliance, certifications, and project case studies
- Improve SEO visibility and generate qualified leads

**Target Audience:** B2B buyers (contractors, consultants, engineers, distributors), project specifiers, government procurement
